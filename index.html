About Us

We are Collaborators, Dreamers, Artists, and Storytellers.

Whale Animation exists to create quality animated content in the form of original series and motion pictures that bring people together, encourage fond memories, and grow dreams.

Why we create

As our attention is continually divided in every direction, capturing time together is increasingly difficult. Nothing holds attention like a good story; they have the power to inspire, educate, motivate, and bond us together. At Whale Animation, we tell these stories with quality, care, and love, bringing people back together to share time in a meaningful way.

Our Mission

Whale Animation strives to make collaboration the forefront of every aspect of our company. We recognize each individual's talents, abilities, and ideas, making for a diverse pool of resources to tell meaningful stories. With an atmosphere of inclusion and open doors, we partner with the best and brightest writers, artists, talent, composers, editors, and staff to create high quality animated content that respects the history that made the genre great and pushes forward into new ideas. Whale Animation will deliver content and stories that inspire, educate, and last - for people of all ages to enjoy.
