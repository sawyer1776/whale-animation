About Us

We are Collaborators, Dreamers, Artists, and most importantly Story Tellers.

Whale Animation exists to create lasting quality family animated content in the form of original series, short films, and feature films that bring people together, plant memories, and grow dreams.

Learn more ➡ close ⬇

Why we create

As the attention of today is continually divided in every direction, capturing time together in meaningful and lasting ways becomes increasingly difficult. Nothing has the power to not only hold attention, but also bring people together; like that of a good story. Stories have the power to inspire, educate, motivate,and bond. At Whale Animation we tell these stories with quality, care, and love, to bring families back together; sharing time in a meaningful way.

Our Mission

Whale Animation strives to make collaboration forefront in every aspect of the company. We recognize each individual's strengths, talents, abilities, and ideas to make for a diverse and strong pool of resources that ultimately exists to make our stories meaningful and lasting. With an atmosphere of inclusion and open doors we seek the brightest and best artists,writers, editors, talent, musicians, and staff to make the highest quality animated content. With respect to the history that made the genre great and the will to go forward into new ideas, Whale Animation will deliver content and stories that inspire, educate, and last; for all ages to enjoy.
